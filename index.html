About Me

My name is Roya Jamshidpour and I am a budding web developer! Before my coding bootcamp at Northwestern University, I had hardly dabbled in code at all. However, with my passion for creativity and problem solving, when I was in search for a career change I turned to coding and never looked back. I am enjoying this process of learning a new skill and look forward to honing them as the months progess. I am also an accomplished jazz vocalist in Chicago- I perform the music I love most, 1920s and '30s jazz, throughout the city and beyond. In my free time I love to rollerblade on the waterfront and go to my musician friends' shows!
